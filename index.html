Real-Time Order Management for Furniture Businesses

A modern cloud platform designed to eliminate lost orders, improve operational visibility, and connect customers, staff, and management through real-time data.

Place an Order Order Management

The Business Challenge

Furniture businesses operate with long production cycles and multiple handover points. Without a central real-time system, orders become difficult to track, updates are missed, and customers lose confidence in the ordering process.

Our Cloud-Based Solution

Customer Order Placement

Customers place orders directly into a shared cloud system, ensuring every order is recorded accurately from the moment it is submitted.

Order Management

Staff manage and update orders through live status changes as they move through production and collection stages.

Operational Visibility

Management gains real-time insight into workload, throughput, and potential bottlenecks through live operational data.

How It Works

Once an order is placed, it appears instantly across the system. As staff update the order status, all changes are synchronised in real time across operational and management views.

Technology Stack

The platform is built using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript on the frontend, Firebase Firestore for real-time cloud data synchronisation, and GitHub Pages for secure and scalable hosting.
